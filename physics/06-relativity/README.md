# Relativity

Physics at high speeds and strong gravity.

## Overview

Einstein's theories of relativity describe physics at speeds near light and in strong gravitational fields.

## Sections

### [Special Relativity](./special-relativity/)
Physics at near-light speeds.
- Lorentz transformations
- Time dilation and length contraction
- Relativistic momentum and energy
- Spacetime diagrams

### [General Relativity](./general-relativity/)
Gravity as spacetime curvature.
- Equivalence principle
- Einstein field equations
- Black holes
- Gravitational waves

## Prerequisites

- Classical mechanics
- Electromagnetism
- Linear algebra
- Differential geometry (for GR)
