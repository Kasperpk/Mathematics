# Particle Physics

Fundamental particles and forces.

## Overview

Particle physics explores the most fundamental constituents of matter and the forces between them.

## Core Topics

### Standard Model
- Quarks (up, down, strange, charm, bottom, top)
- Leptons (electron, muon, tau, neutrinos)
- Gauge bosons (photon, W, Z, gluons)
- Higgs boson

### Fundamental Forces
- Electromagnetic (photon)
- Strong (gluons)
- Weak (W, Z bosons)
- Gravity (graviton?)

### Quantum Field Theory
- Relativistic quantum mechanics
- Feynman diagrams
- Renormalization
- Gauge theories

### Symmetries
- Conservation laws
- CPT symmetry
- Spontaneous symmetry breaking
- Higgs mechanism

### Beyond Standard Model
- Supersymmetry
- Grand unified theories
- String theory
- Dark matter candidates

### Experimental Methods
- Particle accelerators
- Detectors
- Data analysis

## Applications

- Medical imaging
- Radiation therapy
- Materials science
- Computing (WWW invented at CERN)
