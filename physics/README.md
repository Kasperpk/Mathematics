# Physics ⚛️

Understanding the fundamental laws that govern the universe.

## Overview

Physics explores the nature of matter, energy, space, and time. The topics are organized from classical to modern physics.

## Topics

### [01-Classical Mechanics](./01-classical-mechanics/)
Motion, forces, and energy.
- Newtonian mechanics
- Lagrangian and Hamiltonian formulations
- Oscillations and waves

### [02-Thermodynamics](./02-thermodynamics/)
Heat, energy, and entropy.
- Laws of thermodynamics
- Statistical mechanics
- Thermal properties of matter

### [03-Electromagnetism](./03-electromagnetism/)
Electric and magnetic phenomena.
- Electrostatics and magnetostatics
- Maxwell's equations
- Electromagnetic waves

### [04-Waves and Optics](./04-waves-and-optics/)
Wave phenomena and light.
- Wave mechanics
- Geometric optics
- Physical optics

### [05-Quantum Mechanics](./05-quantum-mechanics/)
Physics at the atomic scale.
- Wave-particle duality
- Schrödinger equation
- Quantum states and operators

### [06-Relativity](./06-relativity/)
Physics at high speeds and strong gravity.
- **Special Relativity** - Physics at near-light speeds
- **General Relativity** - Gravity as spacetime curvature

### [07-Nuclear Physics](./07-nuclear-physics/)
Atomic nuclei and nuclear processes.
- Nuclear structure
- Radioactivity
- Nuclear reactions

### [08-Particle Physics](./08-particle-physics/)
Fundamental particles and forces.
- Standard Model
- Particle interactions
- Beyond Standard Model physics

### [09-Astrophysics](./09-astrophysics/)
Physics of celestial objects.
- Stellar physics
- Cosmology
- Black holes and neutron stars
