# Differential Equations

Modeling dynamic systems.

## Overview

Differential equations describe how quantities change over time or space. They are fundamental to modeling physical, biological, and social systems.

## Core Topics

### Ordinary Differential Equations (ODEs)

#### First-Order ODEs
- Separable equations
- Linear equations
- Exact equations
- Integrating factors

#### Higher-Order ODEs
- Constant coefficient equations
- Variation of parameters
- Power series solutions
- Laplace transforms

#### Systems of ODEs
- Matrix methods
- Phase portraits
- Stability analysis

### Partial Differential Equations (PDEs)

#### Classification
- Elliptic (Laplace, Poisson)
- Parabolic (heat equation)
- Hyperbolic (wave equation)

#### Solution Methods
- Separation of variables
- Fourier series
- Green's functions
- Transform methods

### Dynamical Systems
- Phase space
- Fixed points and stability
- Bifurcations
- Limit cycles
- Chaos

## Applications

- Mechanics
- Electromagnetism
- Quantum mechanics
- Heat transfer
- Population dynamics
- Economics
