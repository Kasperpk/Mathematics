The study of life and living organisms.

## Overview

Biology explores life at every scale, from molecules to ecosystems. Topics are organized from molecular to systems level.

## Topics

### [01-Cell Biology](./01-cell-biology/)
The fundamental unit of life.
- Cell structure and organelles
- Cell membrane and transport
- Cell cycle and division
- Cellular signaling

### [02-Genetics](./02-genetics/)
Inheritance and variation.
- Mendelian genetics
- Molecular genetics
- Population genetics
- Epigenetics

### [03-Molecular Biology](./03-molecular-biology/)
Life at the molecular level.
- DNA replication
- Transcription and translation
- Gene regulation
- Genetic engineering

### [04-Evolution](./04-evolution/)
Change over time.
- Natural selection
- Speciation
- Phylogenetics
- Evolutionary developmental biology

### [05-Ecology](./05-ecology/)
Organisms and their environment.
- Population ecology
- Community ecology
- Ecosystem ecology
- Conservation biology

### [06-Microbiology](./06-microbiology/)
Microscopic organisms.
- Bacteria and archaea
- Viruses
- Fungi
- Microbial ecology

### [07-Physiology](./07-physiology/)
Functions of living systems.
- Organ systems
- Homeostasis
- Comparative physiology
- Exercise physiology

### [08-Neuroscience](./08-neuroscience/)
The nervous system and brain.
- Neuroanatomy
- Neurophysiology
- Cognitive neuroscience
- Computational neuroscience

## Connections to Other Sciences

- **Chemistry**: Biochemistry underlies all cellular processes
- **Physics**: Biophysics, biomechanics, thermodynamics in metabolism
- **Mathematics**: Population modeling, biostatistics, bioinformatics
