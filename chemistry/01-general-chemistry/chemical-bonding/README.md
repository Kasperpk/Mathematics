# Chemical Bonding

How atoms connect to form molecules.

## Overview

Chemical bonding explains how atoms combine to form compounds, determining molecular structure and properties.

- Covalent bonds
- Metallic bonds
- Hydrogen bonds
- Van der Waals forces

### Lewis Structures
- Valence electrons
- Octet rule
- Resonance structures
- Formal charge

### VSEPR Theory
- Electron domains
- Molecular geometries
- Bond angles
- Lone pair effects

### Orbital Hybridization
- sp, sp², sp³
- Multiple bonds
- Molecular orbital theory

### Molecular Orbital Theory
- Bonding and antibonding orbitals
- Bond order
- Paramagnetism
- Delocalization

### Intermolecular Forces
- London dispersion forces
- Dipole-dipole interactions
- Hydrogen bonding
- Ion-dipole forces

## Properties Affected by Bonding

- Melting and boiling points
- Solubility
- Conductivity
- Hardness
